My Education Story

I may not have a laundry list of academic achievements, but I did manage to survive my schooling years at KDM School in my hometown Gharaunda - a true test of resilience, if you ask me.
From there, I ventured out into the wild world of college and landed at CGC (Chandigarh Group of Colleges) Landran, Mohali, to pursue my B.Tech in Information Technology. There, I learned a great deal about coding, algorithms, and all things tech - which I promptly forgot as soon as I walked out of the exam hall.
Since then, I've become something of an anti-academic, shunning textbooks and lectures in favor of more exciting pursuits - like binge-watching my favorite shows and arguing with strangers on the internet. I suppose you could say I'm done with formal education, but that doesn't mean I'm done learning. After all, life is the greatest teacher of them all - and so far, it's been one heck of a ride!
